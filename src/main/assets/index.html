WebRTC-Check

WebRTC-Check will test your System WebView component with a mini all-in-one video conferencing app.

What is special: You will be able to see both sides on the same screen.

A real peer-to-peer network connection will be established. The remote camera feed on the right will have a slight lag. The delay is caused by the video stream being encrypted, sent over and being decrypted again. Audio is muted to prevent feedback noises.
